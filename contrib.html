| Do you want to contribute to  |
| PHOENIX? We're looking for... |
+--+-------------------------+--+
   | * Engine Programmers    |
   | * 3D Modeler            |
   | * Animators             |
   | * Comic & Video Artists |
   | * Graphic Artists       |
   | * Concept Artists       |
   +-------------------------+

Engine Programmer: You will be responsible for implementing new features and reconstruct old functions (experience with C++ required), see our Git.

3D Modeler: We look for a 3D artist to model simple low poly ambient objects according to original design concepts and fix some uv-mapping.

Animator: You'll be responsible to create and implement human animations and specific object interactions according to given instructions.

Comic & Video Artist: You will work on spin-offs to our main project and realise teasers, trailers, intros, outros etc.

Graphic Artists: We need you to create low-res textures from alpha versions of GOTHIC and for the textures of Act 2; usually not more than 512x512 pixels. We also look for an artist to create retro sprites and backgrounds for a 2D project.

Concept Artists: We look for concept artists to do some sketches for Act 2 and Act 3 of PHOENIX. This way you can take part in the design of the gloomy city of Khorinis in decay and help us to design dungeons and armors.

If you think you could help us in any other way or give us any kind of support or if you have something that we should add to the Gothic Archive, please reach out:
